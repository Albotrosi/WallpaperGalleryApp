Wallpaper Gallery - Privacy & Data Management

Data Collection Overview

Wallpaper Gallery is committed to transparency regarding data collection and user privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how it's used, and your rights regarding data deletion.

Data We Collect

Wallpaper Gallery App Data:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not track your app usage, browsing history, or personal preferences.
  • No Account Creation: Our app does not require user accounts or login credentials.
  • Local Storage Only: Images are cached locally on your device for performance optimization.

What We Cannot Access:

  • Your personal photos or files
  • Your search queries or browsing history
  • Your device information or location
  • Your contacts or other app data

Third-Party Services & Data

Pixabay API Integration:

Our app uses the Pixabay API to provide wallpaper images. When you use our app, Pixabay may collect certain technical information:

  • IP Address: Pixabay may log your IP address for security and analytics purposes
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device specifications
  • Usage Statistics: Number of images requested and general usage patterns
  • API Requests: Technical data about API calls made through our app

Data Deletion Requests

Wallpaper Gallery Data:

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal information to delete from our systems.

Local App Data:

To remove all locally stored data from our app:

  • Uninstall the App: Remove Wallpaper Gallery from your device
  • Clear App Data: Go to Settings > Apps > Wallpaper Gallery > Storage > Clear Data
  • Clear Cache: This will remove all cached images and temporary files

Pixabay Data:

If you wish to request deletion of data collected by Pixabay:

  • Contact Pixabay directly through their privacy policy page
  • We cannot delete data collected by third-party services
  • Pixabay handles their own data deletion requests

Your Rights

Right to Know:

You have the right to know what data we collect (answer: none) and what data third-party services may collect.

Right to Deletion:

You have the right to request deletion of any data you believe we may have (answer: we have none to delete).

Right to Opt-Out:

You can opt out of data collection by uninstalling the app and avoiding third-party services.

Right to Portability:

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to port or transfer.
